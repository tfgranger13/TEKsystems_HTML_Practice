Is July 4 a national holiday in the United States?

Yes, July 4 has been designated as a federal holiday in the United States. That means that non-essential federal government offices are closed for the day (as are most public companies!).

There are also several other holidays on July 4. Those are National Country Music Day, Sidewalk Egg Frying Day, and National Barbecued Spare Ribs Day.
